## About Green Waste Company

## If it came from the Earth, the Earth needs it back.

We accept all organic materials, including paper products like paper towels, shredded paper, pizza boxes.

Items are donated to our partner farms to help cut feedstock and soil costs. Members of our pickup services receive bi-yearly complimentary compost delivery.
