# Dunkirk District Park Market

## Summary
Thursday afternoons at the entrance to Dunkirk District Park bring Calvert County farms, plant nurseries, honey producers, and food trucks together for the Route 4 commuter crowd. Families stop in for produce, baked goods, and dinner-on-the-go before heading home or to evening ball practices.
