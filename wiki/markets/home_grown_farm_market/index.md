# Home Grown Farm Market

## Summary
Producer-only market inside a red pole-barn pavilion where St. Mary’s farms bring produce, meats, cheeses, baked goods, and flowers to Lexington Park twice a week. Summer Wednesdays add a 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM market window in July and August for midweek shoppers.
