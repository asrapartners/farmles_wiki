## Description

Yellow Green Farmers Market is a dynamic outdoor venue that connects local producers and artisans with the community. In addition to food and artisanal crafts, the market features live music, yoga, art and dance classes as regular happenings. A destination for both locals and tourists, the market is described as "a lush oasis in the heart of an urban landscape." Pets are welcome.
