# North Beach Farmers Market

## Summary
Bayside Saturdays at the North Beach Senior Center lot bring Chesapeake views, fresh produce, flowers, and breakfast bites from Calvert County farms between 8:00 and 11:30 a.m. Families grab coffee, baked goods, and market bouquets while strolling the boardwalk just steps away.
