# Greater Dundalk Farmers Market

## Summary
The Greater Dundalk Chamber set-up turns the corner of Dunmanway and Shipping Place into an early-morning plaza with produce trucks, crab cakes, and church bake tables serving the historic port neighborhood every Saturday.
