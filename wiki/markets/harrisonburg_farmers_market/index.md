# Harrisonburg Farmers Market

## Summary
Year-round growers market at Turner Pavilion featuring Shenandoah Valley farms, bakers, and artisans on Saturdays (and Tuesdays April–Thanksgiving). April through November Tuesdays run 8:00 AM – 1:00 PM along the Turner Pavilion as the weekday companion market.
