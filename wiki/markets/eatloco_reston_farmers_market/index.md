# EatLoco Reston Farmers Market

## Summary
This spring, EatLoco’s newest marketplace..the Halley Rise Market will bloom into a vibrant gathering place where community, creativity, and local entrepreneurship come together in the most inspiring way. Designed to feel welcoming, fresh, and full of discovery, the market will showcase a thoughtfully curated mix of small businesses, artisans, and local makers who bring passion and personality to everything they offer. From unique handcrafted goods to innovative local services, Halley Rise will be more than just a market, it will be a destination where neighbors connect, families explore, and local businesses truly rise. With an atmosphere that celebrates authenticity and supports the spirit of small business, Halley Rise is set to become a signature experience that reflects the heart of the community from day one.
