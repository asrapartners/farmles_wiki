# Blacksburg Farmers Market

## Summary
Downtown Blacksburg’s Market Square pavilion brings Friends of the Farmers Market vendors twice weekly with Appalachian produce, meats, baked goods, and crafts. From April through November a Wednesday 2:00 PM – 6:00 PM market pops up under the same pavilion for midweek staples.
